About Us

Dedicated to Empowering African Rural Women

Established in 1987, the Better Life Program for the African Rural Woman (BLPARW) is a fervent non-profit organisation focused on empowering African rural women. Our mission revolves around enhancing lives through healthcare, fostering economic empowerment, and promoting education.

We channel our efforts through transformative initiatives that include capacity-building, STEM learning, and child sponsorship. Our determination has sparked profound changes, resulting in the successful funding of over 250 projects and the significant enhancement of over 2.4 million lives across three African countries.

Uplift Future Generations

When you choose to sponsor a child through BLPARW, you create far-reaching ripples of change that extend beyond individual lives to entire communities in rural Africa.
