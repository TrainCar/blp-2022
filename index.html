About Us

Better Life Program for the African Rural Woman

BLPARW was founded in 1987 to bring about the emergence of economically empowered African women, living better and participating effectively in national development. Our programs focus on improving access to primary healthcare, capacity building for women, STEM career awareness for girls, and, recently, sponsoring children through their education.

We have funded over 250 projects in 6 countries,

benefiting over 2.4 Million people.
