Empowering Women through Shea Nut Initiative

Economic Upliftment and Enhanced Productivity

Shea nuts provide a vital income for women in rural regions of West and Central Africa, but the labour-intensive nature of harvesting and processing these nuts presents challenges. Through BLPARW's Shea Nut Empowerment Scheme, we are on a mission to support 9,000 women, empowering them with innovative processing techniques to improve their productivity.

This proactive strategy not only elevates their welfare but also amplifies productivity, uncovers opportunities for income growth, and empowers them economically. Consequently, these women are solidifying their significant role in the shea butter supply chain.

Women Empowerment through Multi-Crop Program

Revitalizing Rural Economies

BLPARW's Multi-Crop Empowerment program is a strategic and transformative initiative targeted at elevating the economic prospects of African rural women. Acknowledging the vast potential of infrastructure support, we are actively bolstering the worth of raw agricultural produce via strategic alliances with processing and storage facilities.

As part of our continuous endeavours, we are progressively integrating an ambitious cohort of 100,000 women into regional and global agricultural networks. This orchestrated initiative is simultaneously boosting their income and reinforcing their economic stability, highlighting our unwavering commitment to women's empowerment.

Multi-Trade Empowerment Initiative

Boosting Economic Independence

Our Cooperative Trade Empowerment Initiative stands as a formidable support beacon for over 5,000 traders, fortifying their abilities to form and participate in robust cooperatives. This strategically orchestrated scheme equips traders with superior command over their supply chains, facilitates access to cost-saving opportunities, and promotes enhanced productivity via process optimisation.

Complementing this, our comprehensive entrepreneurial training cultivates indispensable skills for financial autonomy and amplified market access, propelling traders towards enduring prosperity.
